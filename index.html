Proposal: Stabilizing Employee Work Hours

This proposal recommeds reallocating the companies funds and resources to ensure employees receive consistent and sustainable hours.
Providing a stable work schedule will improve employees financial security, moral, and companies performance.

Following the COVID-19 pandemic, many companies redcuded employee hours. While conditions improved, employees continued to experience an inconsistent working schedule

The company should reevaluate unecessary expenditures and reallocate funds towards stabilizing employees work hours.
Areas for potential reallocation could be excessive bonus, short lived concepts, or underperforming contracts.

Investing in employees through stable scheduling is not only responsible but also beneficial.
Work force stability strenghens the company performance and community trust.
