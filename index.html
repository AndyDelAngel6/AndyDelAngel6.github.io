Proposal: Stabilizing Employee Work Hours

This proposal recommends reallocating the company's funds and resources to ensure employees receive consistent and sustainable hours. Providing a stable work schedule will improve employees financial security, morale, and companies performance. Following the COVID-19 pandemic, many companies reduced employee hours. While conditions improved, employees continued to experience an inconsistent working schedule The company should reevaluate unnecessary expenditures and reallocate funds towards stabilizing employees work hours. Areas for potential reallocation could be excessive bonuses, short lived concepts, or underperforming contracts. Investing in employees through stable scheduling is not only responsible but also beneficial. Workforce stability strenghens the company performance and community trust.
